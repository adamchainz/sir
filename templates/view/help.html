Help for the view namespace

URL Description
/view/offloaded_traffic Check how much traffic you would offload with a certain number of prefixes during a period of time.
/view/aggregate_per_as With this view you can aggregate traffic per AS during a period of time.
/view/aggregate_per_prefix With this view you can aggregate traffic per prefix during a period of time.
/view/simulate Given a period of time and a maximum number of prefixes it will simulate how SIR should perform.
